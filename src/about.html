Eva Lendel Story
Charm, mystery, sensuality, romance, all these epithets characterize the brand Eva Lendel, its dresses are the a perfect mix of contemporary details that awaken the desire to create a gentle and at the same time a bright image.
Vision
Eva Lendel is a young brand on the wedding fashion market. It was launched in 2015 with the aim of creating and bringing something new and exclusive to the wedding fashion world. The main idea of the brand is a creation of wedding dresses due to the latest tendencies of world wedding industry.
